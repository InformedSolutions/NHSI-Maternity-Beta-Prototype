Notify national organisations of a qualifying perinatal event

You can use this service to:

  • notify Healthcare Safety Investigation Branch (HSIB) of neonatal, perinatal and maternal deaths and severe brain injuries
  • notify NHS Resolution (NHSR) of severe brain injuries
  • notify Mothers and Babies: Reducing Risk through Audits and Confidential Enquiries (MBBRACE) of neonatal, perinatal and maternal deaths
  • notify your local Child Death Overview Panel (CDOP) through notification to MBBRACE

Important: Notification deadline

You must complete this form within your statuatory deadline.

Before you start

We'll ask you for the relevant information from the following categories:

  • Event details
  • Details or the mother and/or baby involved
  • Information about the trust
